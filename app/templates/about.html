About AI Career Guidance

Empowering Mount Kigali University students with intelligent career guidance for a brighter future.

Our Mission

The AI Career Guidance System is a smart digital tool designed specifically for Mount Kigali University (MKU) to help students choose suitable career paths based on their interests, academic performance, and skills.

Our main role is to provide personalized career recommendations that guide students toward professions where they are most likely to succeed. Integrated into MKU's student portal and career services department, we bridge the gap between student potential and career fulfillment.

The system addresses the common problem of career confusion, where students struggle to match their abilities with the right academic and professional paths, while also solving the issue of limited access to one-on-one career counseling.

Who We Serve

The primary users of the system are MKU students, along with career counselors and academic advisors who support them. The system is typically used during student orientation, course selection periods, and before graduation.

By analyzing student data, our AI provides reliable recommendations that support informed decision-making. Validation at MKU is done through student feedback, counselor reviews, and tracking career outcomes, ensuring that the guidance provided is accurate and effective.
